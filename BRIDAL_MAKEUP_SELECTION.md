# Bridal Makeup Image Selection Guide

## Based on Your Instagram Profile (@jmk_beauty_salon)

### Your Profile Shows:
- **Profile Picture**: Traditional South Asian bride with:
  - Elaborate makeup (dark eyeliner, defined brows, nude lip)
  - Gold and emerald jewelry (maang tikka, jhumkas, choker)
  - Gold dupatta with intricate detailing
  - Professional, elegant look

### What Images to Select:

#### Priority 1: Traditional Bridal Looks (3-4 images)
- Similar to your profile picture style
- Gold jewelry combinations
- Traditional makeup styles
- Complete bridal transformations

#### Priority 2: Modern Bridal Looks (2-3 images)
- Contemporary makeup styles
- Fusion looks
- Different color palettes
- Modern jewelry combinations

#### Priority 3: Detail Shots (1-2 images)
- Close-up makeup details
- Eye makeup close-ups
- Lip details
- Jewelry details

## Selection Criteria:

### ✅ Choose Images That:
1. Show complete bridal looks (not just makeup)
2. Have good lighting and clarity
3. Showcase your best work
4. Have variety in styles
5. Are from your most popular posts

### ❌ Avoid Images That:
1. Are blurry or low quality
2. Show incomplete looks
3. Have poor lighting
4. Are too similar to each other

## Step-by-Step Process:

### 1. Review Your Instagram Posts
- Go to: https://www.instagram.com/jmk_beauty_salon/
- Filter by posts with bridal makeup
- Look for posts with hashtags: #bride, #bridalmakeup

### 2. Select Top 5-8 Images
- Choose your absolute best work
- Mix of traditional and modern
- Different angles and styles

### 3. Download Images
- Save to: `client/public/images/portfolio/bridal-makeup/`
- Use descriptive names

### 4. Update Portfolio
- Use the script or update database manually
- Make sure featured images are your best ones
